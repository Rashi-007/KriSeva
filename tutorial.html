Quick Farming Tips

  • Test your soil regularly to understand its nutrient content.
  • Use organic compost to improve soil fertility.
  • Implement crop rotation to reduce pests and diseases.
  • Install drip irrigation systems to conserve water.
  • Keep up with market trends to sell your produce at better prices.
